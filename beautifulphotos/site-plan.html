Overview

Purpose

Hi, I’m Jarom Costales, I am a programmer and I also love photography. I have worked on several projects of compiling and editing photos and videos before. I want to create a website where people can store and find photos and videos that they can use everywhere and are free for commercial use. They can use it on their website, blog, product or anywhere else. It will be a library that is donated by a global community of photographers who are willing to share their work for everyone to use freely.

Do you like browsing the web to find cool photos and videos that you can use everywhere? If so, then this is a website you should definitely bookmark. It hosts collections of photos and videos of nature, portrait, abstract, landscape, sports and many other things. These photos and videos are shared by amazing photographers from all around the world. Plus, it’s a great place to get inspiration for ideas as well, whether you’re thinking about becoming a photographer too.

Audience

People from all over the world

Home page

This will be a website where people can store and find photos and videos that they can use everywhere and are free for commercial use. They can use it on their website, blog, product or anywhere else. It will be a library that is donated by a global community of photographers who are willing to share their work for everyone to use freely. It will kind of be like google photos with a mix of canvas. It will be a storage place for photos and videos, and if they are willing, they can allow other people to see their storage. The homepage will look something like this:

[Page 2]

People will come to my website because we now live in a world where taking photos is very accessible. In fact, I believe most people like taking photos everywhere they go or in whatever they do and with that, they need a place where they can store those photos and videos. And my website will be a huge help for them. Also, it will not just be for people who wants to store photos but also for those people who want to take pictures online for their website, school project, wallpaper, product or any other.
